About Research Triangle Park

Research Triangle Park (RTP) is one of the largest research parks in the world, spanning over 7,000 acres in the heart of North Carolina. Founded in 1959, RTP has grown into a thriving community that brings together leading technology companies, world-class universities, and innovative research institutions.

The "Triangle" refers to the three major research universities that form the corners of the region: Duke University in Durham, North Carolina State University in Raleigh, and the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. This unique collaboration between academia and industry has made RTP a global leader in technology, biotechnology, and research.
