Major Industries

Research Triangle Park is home to diverse industries that drive innovation and economic growth. The park's economy is built on a foundation of technology, biotechnology, and research.

Technology Sector

RTP is a major technology hub, with companies specializing in software development, networking, cloud computing, and artificial intelligence. Major tech companies like IBM, Cisco, and Lenovo have significant operations in the park.

Biotechnology & Pharmaceuticals

The biotechnology and pharmaceutical industries are major economic drivers in RTP. Companies conduct cutting-edge research in drug development, medical devices, and life sciences, contributing to medical breakthroughs that improve lives worldwide.

Research & Development

Many companies choose RTP specifically for its research and development capabilities. The proximity to three major research universities provides access to top talent and collaborative research opportunities.

Major Companies

Research Triangle Park hosts some of the world's most recognized companies across various industries.

  • IBM - Technology and research
  • Cisco Systems - Networking and technology
  • Lenovo - Computer technology
  • GlaxoSmithKline - Pharmaceuticals
  • RTI International - Research institute
  • National Institute of Environmental Health Sciences - Government research
  • Red Hat - Open source software (now part of IBM)
  • Many startups and growing companies

Universities: The Foundation of RTP

The three major universities that form the "Triangle" are essential to RTP's economic success. They provide talent, research, and innovation that attract companies to the region.

Duke University

Located in Durham, Duke University is a world-renowned research institution known for excellence in medicine, engineering, and business. Duke's research and graduates contribute significantly to RTP's economy.

University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill

UNC-Chapel Hill is the nation's first public university and a leading research institution. Its programs in health sciences, business, and technology provide talent and research that benefit RTP companies.

North Carolina State University

NC State, located in Raleigh, is known for its strong engineering, technology, and agricultural programs. The university's focus on applied research and innovation makes it a natural partner for RTP companies.

Job Opportunities

RTP offers diverse career opportunities across many fields. The region consistently ranks among the best places in the country for job growth and career opportunities.

High-Demand Fields:

  • Software Engineering and Development
  • Biotechnology and Life Sciences
  • Data Science and Analytics
  • Research and Development
  • Project Management
  • Business and Finance

The combination of major companies, startups, and research institutions creates a dynamic job market with opportunities for professionals at all stages of their careers.

Economic Impact

Research Triangle Park's economic impact extends far beyond its boundaries. The park generates billions of dollars in economic activity, supports thousands of jobs, and contributes to North Carolina's reputation as a leader in innovation and technology. The collaboration between universities and industry creates a unique economic ecosystem that benefits the entire region.
